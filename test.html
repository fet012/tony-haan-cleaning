About us

At Tony Haan Entrümpelung & Glass and Gebäudereinigung, we use modern tools, professional equipment, and eco-friendly products to deliver top-quality cleaning services. Our team of trained professionals is dedicated to transforming your spaces into pristine environments.

From deep sofa and carpet cleaning to high-reach window washing and powerful facade restoration, our advanced methods ensure every space is thoroughly cleaned and refreshed. We combine German precision with innovative cleaning techniques to achieve exceptional results.

We specialize in household clearances, scrap removal, and decluttering with speed and care, making your environment cleaner, safer, and more comfortable. Whether it's a residential property or commercial space, we handle each project with the same level of professionalism and attention to detail.
